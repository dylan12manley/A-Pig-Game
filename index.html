A Pig Game

Rules:

P1 rolls a die, if it lands on a 1, it is the other players turn. P1 gets 0 points
If P1 rolls anything except for a 1, P1 can either hold, ending their turn
and adding the current roll value to their total score, or P1 can roll again.
Or P1 can choose to roll again. If the seccond roll is a 1, p1's turn is over
and gets 0 points added to their total score.
A player can keep rolling and agrigating turn points, but
most hold before rolling a 1 for those points to count as total points.
First player to 100 points wins!
